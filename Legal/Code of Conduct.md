# OpenEnergy Contributor Covenant

## Code of Conduct

Welcome to the OpenEnergy community.

OpenEnergy is built to advance simple, efficient, and practical energy systems through open learning, respectful collaboration, and responsible innovation.

This Code of Conduct helps ensure that all contributors work together in a safe, constructive, and progress-driven environment.

---

## 🌍 Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in OpenEnergy welcoming, respectful, and fair for everyone acting in good faith.

We value contributors of all backgrounds who support the mission of improving open energy technologies for education, experimentation, and community progress.

We commit to maintaining a space where ideas can grow through collaboration, discipline, and mutual respect.

---

## ⚡ Our Purpose

OpenEnergy exists to support progress in:

* Direct energy systems
* Renewable hardware learning
* Open engineering collaboration
* Practical experimentation
* Efficient and simple energy design
* Community-driven innovation

All participation should align with these goals.

---

## ✅ Encouraged Behaviors

Contributors are encouraged to:

* Respect other members and their time
* Share knowledge openly and responsibly
* Give constructive technical feedback
* Credit sources and contributors properly
* Help improve documentation and designs
* Focus discussions on useful progress
* Accept feedback professionally
* Support a healthy learning environment

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behaviors

The following are not permitted:

* Harassment or intimidation
* Personal attacks or insults
* Discrimination or hate speech
* Misleading claims or impersonation
* Sharing confidential or stolen material
* Repeated disruptive arguments
* Spam or unrelated promotion
* Unsafe technical advice given irresponsibly
* Any conduct that harms community trust

---

## 🛠 Project-Focused Expectations

To help OpenEnergy progress, contributors should aim to provide:

* Tested engineering ideas
* Useful documentation improvements
* Practical design refinements
* Honest data and measurements
* Safety-minded suggestions
* Educational value for future users

---

## 📩 Reporting Issues

If you experience or observe behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainers through the official repository channels or listed contact methods.

Reports will be reviewed fairly and respectfully.

---

## ⚖ Enforcement

Depending on severity, moderators may take actions including:

1. Friendly reminder
2. Warning
3. Temporary restriction
4. Temporary suspension
5. Permanent removal from project spaces

The goal of enforcement is to protect progress, safety, and community well-being.

---

## 🌱 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* GitHub repositories
* Issues and pull requests
* Discussions and chats
* Community events
* Public spaces where OpenEnergy is represented

---

## 🤝 Final Principle

OpenEnergy is built for progress.

Respectful people build great systems together.
